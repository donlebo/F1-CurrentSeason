Formula 1 constructors are companies specialized in the design, construction and development of racing cars for competition in the top automotive series.
Every year, the constructors compete in the Formula 1 World Championship, trying to achieve the greatest possible success.
In addition to the design and construction of the cars, the Formula 1 constructors are also responsible for their ongoing development, both during and between seasons.
